# Executive Overview

## What Hollow House Institute Does

Hollow House Institute (HHI) focuses on operational AI governance infrastructure.

The work is centered on helping organizations improve:
- AI oversight
- governance visibility
- accountability continuity
- audit readiness
- operational governance workflows

Rather than treating governance as static policy documentation, HHI focuses on governance during system operation.

This includes:
- runtime oversight
- governance telemetry
- escalation visibility
- behavioral drift analysis
- replay-capable evidence
- longitudinal accountability
