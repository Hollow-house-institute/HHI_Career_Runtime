# 90 Day Governance Plan

## First 30 Days

Focus:
- understand operational workflows
- identify governance surfaces
- map AI usage patterns
- document oversight gaps
- identify escalation failures
- review accountability structures

Deliverables:
- governance surface inventory
- AI workflow map
- escalation pathway review
- operational risk observations
- governance documentation baseline

---

## Days 31–60

Focus:
- improve governance visibility
- strengthen oversight workflows
- establish telemetry structures
- improve evidence continuity
- clarify Decision Boundaries

Deliverables:
- governance workflow recommendations
- escalation mapping
- telemetry structure recommendations
- oversight improvement recommendations
- governance evidence templates

---

## Days 61–90

Focus:
- operationalize governance processes
- improve accountability continuity
- strengthen audit readiness
- establish governance reporting cadence
- support longitudinal oversight

Deliverables:
- governance operations summary
- audit readiness documentation
- governance reporting structure
- operational governance recommendations
- longitudinal accountability recommendations

---

## Governance Philosophy

Governance should remain operational during execution, not only during policy creation.

Core doctrine:

Time turns behavior into infrastructure.  
Behavior is the most honest data there is.

Telemetry proves operation.  
Policies describe intent.
