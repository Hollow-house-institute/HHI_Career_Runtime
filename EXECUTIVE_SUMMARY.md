# Executive Summary

Amy Pierce Bui is the founder of Hollow House Institute (HHI), focused on operational AI governance infrastructure, execution-time governance, governance telemetry, and behavioral governance systems.

Current work centers on runtime governance for AI systems operating across distributed, interoperable, and multi-agent environments.

Primary operational focus areas include:
- governance documentation
- runtime oversight
- governance telemetry
- behavioral drift analysis
- audit-ready governance evidence
- longitudinal accountability
- standards interoperability
- governance workflow analysis
