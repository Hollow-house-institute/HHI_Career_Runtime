# What Is HHI?

Hollow House Institute (HHI) focuses on Behavioral AI Governance and Execution-Time Governance.

The core focus is understanding how AI systems behave during real operational use over time, not just how they are described in policies or design documents.

HHI emphasizes:
- runtime governance
- behavioral drift detection
- governance telemetry
- Decision Boundary enforcement
- Stop Authority preservation
- longitudinal accountability
- human-in-the-loop oversight

---

# Core Thesis

Policies describe intent.

Runtime behavior reveals reality.

Time turns behavior into infrastructure.

Behavior is the most honest data there is.
